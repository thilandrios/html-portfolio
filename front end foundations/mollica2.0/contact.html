Meet The Team

David Mollica – Executive Director, Chairman of the Board

David was inspired to start Kid Careers during his time as an Americorps volunteer at a local elementary school. There, he realized that there was an unfulfilled need for open access career resources written at the elementary school level. David holds a M.A. degree from the University of London and currently resides near Altoona, PA.

Todd McKeone - Secretary

Todd McKeone has lived in Central Pennsylvania for nearly all his 50 years, and has always sought to find new ways to contribute to the development of the area. He graduated from Hollidaysburg Area High School in 1988, and served four years in the Army. Todd received a B.A. in English from Penn State in 2005.



Todd has served on the Blair County Chamber of Commerce’s Buy Here Live Here Committee and has served on the board of Taking Back Tomorrow, which was an organization dedicated to cleaning up the Altoona area. Todd is currently the membership coordinator for the Altoona Area Business Community and is the director of the Route 22 Rock and Blues Festival. Todd founded ActiveAltoona, LLC in 2020 and published ActiveAltoona.com as a resource to raise awareness of local events for both tourists and residents of Central Pennsylvania.



Adam Roth - Treasurer

Adam has a long history of working with youth and disadvantaged groups. His experience ranges from working with kids as a church youth director, providing support services to the intellectually disabled, and working closely with nonprofits to coordinate volunteers at his current job. Adam has a background in psychology and youth ministry. Adam believes in the vision that has been set in place for Kid Careers and can’t wait to see it grow.
